Geometry reference.

Court aspect         44 / 20   (real pickleball court, feet)
Kitchen band width   15.9%   (7 / 44)
Kitchen L/R starts   34.1%   (from each side)
Net X                50%
Centerline width     34.1%   (each half · 0→34.1% and 65.9%→100%)
Centerline Y         50%
Line thickness       2px  kitchen + center,  4px  net
Border               2px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.95)

Colors are from tokens.css · R19 NEPC palette.
Never hardcode — always reference --nepc-* tokens.
